A Letter from Our Founder

Hi there—Bob here. If you’d told me 20 years ago that I’d pour my hard-earned savings into Cluff Natural Resources stock based on chat-forum hype and watch it evaporate down to just £0.17, I wouldn’t have believed you. But that’s exactly what happened—I invested thousands, endured the day-trading rollercoaster (and lost twice as fast), and felt overwhelmed by conflicting “expert” advice. Approaching 50, I don’t have decades to figure it all out.

I realized there had to be a better way:

  • Cut the noise of endless menus and opinions that contradict themselves.
  • Show you what works—no more “trust me” strategies.
  • Give you real numbers on 4,000+ stocks so you can decide for yourself.

That’s why I built BacktestBob: a one-click backtesting tool that runs proven strategies across decades of data and thousands of tickers. No code. No spreadsheets. Just “proof over promises”—see what really works.

If you’re done with financial FOMO and forum drama—welcome home. I’ll be right there with you: sharing my own backtests, celebrating wins, and learning from misses—together.

— Bob
Founder of BacktestBob
